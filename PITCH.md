# StellarCarry Pitch Deck

## Slide 1: Problem
**"I want something from Japan, but shipping is $60 and customs is a nightmare."**

Cross-border shopping is broken:
- High shipping costs (often exceeding item value)
- Customs complexity & delays
- No trust between strangers
- Zero accountability when things go wrong

## Slide 2: Solution
**"A traveler from Tokyo is flying to Istanbul next week. They buy it, bring it, get paid."**

StellarCarry connects **buyers** who want foreign products with **travelers** who have luggage space — all secured by **Soroban smart contracts on Stellar**.

## Slide 3: How It Works
1. **Buyer posts** a request: "iPhone case from Tokyo → Istanbul, 50 XLM reward"
2. **Traveler accepts** and funds locked in escrow
3. **Traveler delivers** item
4. **Buyer confirms** delivery → escrow releases to traveler
5. **Reputation** recorded on-chain for future matches

## Slide 4: Technology
- **Stellar Soroban** smart contracts for trustless escrow

## Slide 5: Market Size
- $2.4T global cross-border e-commerce by 2025
- 1.4B international tourist arrivals annually
- Every traveler has unused luggage allowance
- P2P delivery = 60-80% cheaper than traditional shipping

## Slide 6: Competitive Advantage
| Feature | StellarCarry | Traditional |
|---------|-------------|-------------|
| Trust | Smart contract escrow | PayPal (centralized) |
| Fees | Near-zero (Stellar) | 3-5% + FX markup |
| Reputation | On-chain, permanent | Platform-dependent |
| Settlement | Instant (3-5 sec) | 3-5 business days |
